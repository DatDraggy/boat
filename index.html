An exclusive Boat party departing from the Estrel

Hot Summer Nights is an exclusive party on a large boat. From the Estrel dock to the Spree and back, through the German Capital, take in the sunset and mingle on the upper deck, retreat to the lounge and dancefloor with live DJs.

The Party

The first edition of Hot Summer Nights will depart at 19:00 CEST, starting from the Estrel hotel. For 4 hours we will entertain you with music, events and a beautiful sightseeing over the Spree river in Berlin.

Location

The location is one of the larger ships of the Stern und Kreis Company. All ships are equipped with a bar, outdoor area and plenty of space to chill, dance and suit.

Drinks

On the boat we have a fully equipped bar with all kinds of drinks available. Want a drink after fursuiting or dancing? No problem! But keep in mind that the bar takes cash only and is operated by the ship company – You can't bring your own drinks.

Fursuits

Do boats and fursuits work? Of course they do! We make sure that it's very easy to bring your stuff on the boat, and we will have a fursuit lounge with basic supplies. Water for suiters is provided, but please don't bring your own drinks. We do understand that you maybe don't want to suit during the whole trip. Simply change out of suit and we keep guard.

Transport

For this party you don't have to travel at all! The boat will depart from the Estrel, at the beergarden pier. Also it will bring us all back there. Please mind that check-in (getting your badge and goodies) will be done before boarding and also close to the hotel. More info about that will be released later.

Charity

All profits we make will be donated to the charity of Eurofurence. These are profits from the normal tickets, but especially the VIP upgrade can help the charity a lot.

We wanted to do something special for celebrating the 25th edition of Eurofurence and the 5th edition in Berlin, the cities that knows how to party! So why not on a boat? We can show you around Berlin via the beautiful Spree river and in the mean time chill, party and suit up. Basically we have a fursuitwalk (without walking) and party in one. Throw in a beautiful sunset and the best DJs and it's a perfect experience you don't want to miss.
