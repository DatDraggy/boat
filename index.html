Get ready for the booty! The pirates are here!

All Paws on Deck! The exclusive boat party is back! Now with TWO parties to choose out of! Whether you’re looking for a leisurely afternoon sightseeing cruise or a floating night club, we aim to offer a unique experience for anyone. Have a drink, take in the sunset and chill on the upper deck or retreat to the lounge and dance with one of our numerous live DJs.

1 Boat, 2 Parties

To be able to accommodate the lot of you comfortably, we have had to extend our program. Let’s set sail to the unknown! On the 18th of August, we will go on 2 adventures with our own White Pearl. At 13:00 CET we will explore the city of Berlin in search of coin and gold.

Later that day we will depart from the Estrel at 19:00 CET for an evening party cruise on the wild seas! Our wish is to give as many of you a chance to join our trip. Therefore attendees may only register for one of the two parties, this is due to high demand.

Location

Our vessel of choice, the Mark Brandenburg is the biggest ship of the Stern und Kreis Company. With 2 bars, 2 outdoor areas, a fursuit lounge and plenty of space to chill, dance and suit our flagship is the perfect place to start Eurofurence 26!

Please keep in mind that it is a ship suitable for cities. That means that the ceiling is low (around 2 meters high) and the outdoor decks are not always available due to low bridges.

Transport

The afternoon cruise (Party A | 13:00 CET) will depart from and return to Treptower Park (maps link). This is to save time getting to the city center. The boarding location in at Treptower Park is one subway stop away from the Berlin-Sonnenallee subway stop (maps link). Badge pickup will take place prior to boarding at the hotel.

The evening cruise (Party B | 19:00 CET) will depart from and return to the Estrel beergarden pier. Badge pickup will take place prior to boarding at the hotel.

Fursuits

Want to bring your fursuit? No problem! We will provide a fursuit lounge with all the necessary supplies. We do understand that you maybe don't want to suit during the whole trip, EF security will keep guard and make sure your suit stays safe and sound during your trip.

Drinks

Getting thirsty from all the dancing? We got you covered! Our ship has 2 bars with all kinds of drinks available for you. he bar accepts cash and card payments and is operated by the shipping company.

Charity

All revenue from the VIP tickets will be donated directly to the charity of Eurofurence 26. That means that for every VIP upgrade 15 euros will be transferred directly!

Registration

After your registration was approved by our staff your place on the boat is reserved. Once payment is due, you will receive an email from the Eurofurence Reg System.

The VIP upgrade is our name for the sponsor upgrade. With this upgrade you do not only get special goodies, but you also support the Eurofurence charity! All proceeds from the VIP upgrade go directly to charity.

The upgrade is not limited to a maximum amount and can always be bought later in the user area. Downgrading is not possible but transferring it to someone else is. Just send us an email.

Before you register it is important that you are familiar with our Code of Conduct and Terms and Conditions. They explain the rules for keeping it a fun and awesome party.

Want to see who is going to party hard with us? Check out our Attendees List!

If you have any questions or can't wait until the party is starting, then join our Telegram group @summerboat.

FAQ

In 2019 some friends wanted to do something special for celebrating the 25th edition of Eurofurence and the 5th edition in Berlin, the city that knows how to party! So why not on a boat? Cruising on the beautiful Spree River through Berlin. We wanted a chill atmosphere for partying and suiting up. The double-decker-ship Mark Brandenburg offered us just that. Live DJs would provide great dance vibes and the upper would allow for alluring views. It did! And now we’re back for more!

You can purchase a Normal ticket for €35. We also offer a VIP upgrade for additional €15. Not only will this include a special gift and a special VIP badge, but you also help us to make the party even more awesome!

The chance of getting seasick on this boat is very minimal. The Mark Brandenburg ist one of this biggest ships out there and the speed and the size of the boat make it impossible to feel any waves from other boats on the Spree. We do advise to talk with your doctor in case you get seasick very easily. We expect smooth sailing!
