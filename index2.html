An exclusive boat party departing from the Estrel

All Paws on Deck is an exclusive party on a large boat. From the Estrel dock to the Spree and back, through the German Capital, take in the sunset and mingle on the upper deck, retreat to the lounge and dancefloor with live DJs.

The Party

The All Paws on Deck 2020 Boat will depart at 19:00 CEST, starting from the Estrel hotel. For 4 hours we will entertain you with music, events and a beautiful sightseeing over the Spree river in Berlin.

Location

The location is one of the larger ships of the Stern und Kreis Company. All ships are equipped with a bar, outdoor area and plenty of space to chill, dance and suit.

Drinks

On the boat we have a fully equipped bar with all kinds of drinks available. Want a drink after fursuiting or dancing? No problem! The bar accepts cash and card payments and is operated by the ship company – You can't bring your own drinks.

Fursuits

Do boats and fursuits work? Of course they do! We make sure that it's very easy to bring your stuff on the boat, and we will have a fursuit lounge with basic supplies. Water for suiters is provided, but please don't bring your own drinks. We do understand that you maybe don't want to suit during the whole trip. Simply change out of suit and we keep guard.

Transport

For this party you don't have to travel at all! The boat will depart from the Estrel, at the beergarden pier. Also it will bring us all back there. Please mind that check-in (getting your badge and goodies) will be done before boarding and also close to the hotel. More info about that will be released later.

Charity

All profits we make will be donated to the charity of Eurofurence. These are profits from the normal tickets, but especially the VIP upgrade can help the charity a lot.

Registration

After your registration was approved by our staff your place on the boat is reserved. Once payment is due, you will receive an email from the Eurofurence Reg System.

The VIP upgrade is our name for the good old sponsor upgrade. With this upgrade you not only get special goodies, but you also support us so that we can make it the best party ever! The upgrade is not limited to a maximum amount and can always be bought later in the user area. Downgrading is not possible but transferring it to someone else is. Just send us an email.

Before you register it is important that you are familiar with our Code of Conduct and Terms and Conditions. They explain the rules for keeping it a fun and awesome party.

Want to see who is going to party hard with us? Check out our Attendees List!

If you have any questions or can't wait until the party is starting, then join our Telegram group @summerboat.

FAQ

We wanted to do something special for celebrating the 25th edition of Eurofurence and the 5th edition in Berlin, the city that knows how to party! So why not on a boat? We can show you around Berlin via the beautiful Spree river and in the mean time chill, party and suit up. Basically we have a fursuitwalk (without walking) and party in one. Throw in a beautiful sunset and the best DJs and it's a perfect experience you don't want to miss.

You can purchase a Normal ticket for €35. We also offer a VIP upgrade for additional €15. You get a special gift and a special VIP badge, but you also help us to make the party even more awesome.
