Afternoon event

General information

Join us on our White Pearl through the city of Berlin. Starting from Treptower Park, we will be on a 4 hour trip to the center of Berlin and back. Get a drink at the bar and enjoy the sun while listening to our DJ’s.

Just like the evening party we will have a dancefloor where you can dance all day long. Want to suit up? No problem! The fursuitlounge downstairs is of course also available with all the stuff you need.

This event is perfect for fursuiters and photographers or for the people who want to start Eurofurence the relaxing way.

Meet and Greet Berlin

During the trip we will also have a one hour photo stop in the center of Berlin. Perfect to take pictures in fursuit or to just walk around a bit. The stop will be at the Berliner Dom, next to the museum island. During this stop you are welcome to get off board or stay on the ship.

If you go off board, please read the tips below:

  • We won’t wait for you when leaving. Too late is too late. We will announce the exact departure time before docking.
  • Fursuiting near the Dom is possible, but we advice to bring someone with you because it can be busy with tourists and people enjoying the sun.
  • EF Security stays near the ship to make sure that non attendees won’t enter the ship. So please bring your badge with you when going off board.
  • Please head to any of the staff when you have any questions.

Transportation/How to get there

The event will start and end at Treptower Park, next to S-Bahn station Treptower Park. There are different ways to get there.

By public transportation

Walk from the Estrel to S-Bahn station Sonnenallee. From there take the S42 for 1 stop to Treptower Park. This train goes every 10 minutes. At Treptower Park you can follow the signs towards the ship. It will be a 5 minute walk to the pier. For this trip you can buy a Short Trip ticket (Kurzstrecke). A one way ticket costs €1,70.

By car/taxi

While we prefer that most people come by public transportation cause it’s fast and easy, we also understand that it is not possible for everyone. From the Estrel it is a 10 minute ride to the nearest parking spot next to Treptower Park. Navigation address: Puschkinallee 15, 12435 Berlin

Parking is free and it’s a 5 minute walk from the parking to the ship. You can find the parking on the right hand side when you’re almost near the navigation address. There will be a sign for parking Treptower Hafen.
If you use a taxi, let them drive you to S-Bahn station Treptower Park. From there it is a 5 minute walk.

Shuttle Busses

At this moment we are still looking into the option to get a few shuttle busses from the Estrel to the ship. This will mostly be for the fursuiters. We will update with more info in the coming months.

Evening party

Party from begin to end!

Want to start Eurofurence with an awesome party? Look no further! During this 4 hour party we will make sure you dance your butt off with the best DJ’s of Eurofurence. You can also enjoy the sunset over Berlin on the outdoor decks or use our silent disco to even party outside!

We won’t forget about the fursuiters, we will bring a fully equipped fursuitlounge where you can change into your fursuit or store it when not needed. More events are added in the coming months!

Transportation/How to get there

The evening party starts and ends right in front of the Estrel so no need to travel far! Just go to the beergarden and hop on!

Badge Pickup

Badge pickup will be on Monday from 15:00 CET till 21:00 CET and on Tuesday from 09:00 CET till 17:00 CET. The pickup is for both parties. Just keep in mind that from Tuesday 12:30 CET you can only pick up badges for the evening party.
